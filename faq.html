FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)
Q: I decided to postpone my MCQ exam, can I attend the next MCQ Prep Course offered by PharmaSpirit?
PharmaSpirit does offer a retake course, however this only applies to those who fail the MCQ exam after attending our prep course. For those who postpone the exam, PharmaSpirit will extend your online access at no cost. If you would like to re-attend the MCQ lectures the next time the prep course is offered than there is a fee $600+tax. The fee does not include updated materials.
Q: Does PharmaSpirit offer discounts?
For the OSCE Prep Course, if you have taken the MCQ Prep course with us, then you will receive $100 off the OSCE Prep Coure fee. Discounts can only be obtained at the time of registration.
Q: When is the course offered?
Most of our classes are held on the weekend, however, sometimes we may need to hold classes during the week. PharmaSpirit tries to accommodate all its student’s schedule. We know it’s tough to please everyone, but that does not mean we don’t try. Our instructors are available 7 days a week by phone or email. Students can contact their instructors at any time with any questions and concerns they may have.
Q: Is there a registration deadline?
As long as there is available space in the program, students are welcomed to register up until the course start date. Enrollment is based on a first-come-first serve basis.
Q: The MCQ prep course begins before my evaluating exam results are out, how can I take the course if I pass my EE?
We do have a special option for those waiting for their evaluating results. If you are waiting for your EE results, contact us before our classes begin and let us know. We will do our best to accommodate your situation. However if you wait until after you receive your result to contact us we can not guarantee you a spot in the course
Q: What happens after the application has gone through and my fees are paid?
Once you have completed the registration process, you will receive an email informing you that course materials have been mailed out to you. You will also receive a PharmaSpirit username and password, which allows you to access our online videos available to you on the PharmaSpirit website. In addition, your course instructor will be in touch with you via email to introduce him/herself to you, provide you with more details about the course and provide you with an attachment of the course schedule. Once you receive your materials you are welcome to begin the readings immediately.
Q: How do I apply?
Go to www.pharmaspirit.com/registration and choose your method. You have 2 options: 1) Click on HERE if you prefer to pay by cash, cheque or e-transfer and fill out the registration form online. Please make cheques payable to PharmaSpirit Consulting Inc. and have it mail to our address at 5800 Ambler Dr. suite 210, Mississauga, ON L4W 4J4. To e-transfer your fee, please contact us for details how 2) Click on Register to pay by credit card or paypal online and fill out the registration form.
Q: What do I do if I miss classes?
PharmaSpirit does not offer make up classes, but what we do offer is a guarantee that the instructor will be available to answer any question the student has in order to ensure that the student understands the material he/she has already read. This is not a repeat of the class, but only a question/answer period.
Q: What if I fail the MCQ exam, do you have offer a free re-take course?
Yes. If, after you take our course, you fail the MCQ exam, PharmaSpirit offers you one free re-take course. You will attend all the lectures online and have access to the online material. You will NOT, however, receive the updated materials for the new course. New materials can be purchased for $200 + tax.
Q: What is the refund policy?
In the case where a student requires a course cancellation 100% of the money will be refunded, as long as the cancellation occurs prior to the printing of course materials. If your materials have been printed then $200 will be deducted from the refundable fee. As soon as your materials are printed, we will email you to let you know. If, however, you receive the materials the fee is non-refundable. You do have the option to postpone your attendance to the next course session. For example, if you register for MCQ January session and cannot make it, you may carry over your fee to the MCQ August session.
Q: Is there any homework?
Students will be expected to complete pre-readings, as outlined in the schedule. Once you have registered, your instructor will email each student with a course outline that they are expected to follow. Finishing the required work will help you develop the skills you need to pass the qualifying exam with confidence.
